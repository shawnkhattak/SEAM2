# Risk Flags

Version 1 risk flag types:

- Sanctions Match
- Possible Sanctions Match
- High-Risk Flag Country
- Risky Ownership Connection
- Negative News Mention
- Singapore Port Activity Match
- Unknown Ownership
- Conflicting Identity Data
- Missing IMO Number
- Missing MMSI Number

## Rule

Risk flags should be transparent and evidence-backed. AI can explain risk flags but must not invent or create sanctions flags by itself.
